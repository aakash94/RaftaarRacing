ABOUT
THE TEAM.

Raftaar Racing is a student-led F1 in Schools team obsessed with pushing the limits of what young engineers can design, build, and race.

Our philosophy is simple: Speed is a science, and we are the scientists. From computational fluid dynamics to precision CNC machining, every millimeter of our car is optimized for the win.
