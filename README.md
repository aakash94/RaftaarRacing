# 🏎️ Welcome to the Raftaar Racing Website!

Hey there! This is **your team's website**. You can change the words, swap the photos, and keep it looking awesome — all from the GitHub website. No software to install. No coding knowledge needed. Just you and your browser.

---

## The most important rule 📌

You only ever need to touch **two folders**:

| Folder | What it holds |
|---|---|
| ✏️ `content/` | All the **words** on your site |
| 🖼️ `images/` | All the **pictures** on your site |

**Everything else** (the `assets/` folder, `index.html`, and any other files) is the grown-up machinery that makes the site work. Leave those alone and everything will be fine.

---

## The 3 things you'll do most

### ✏️ Change some words

1. Go into the `content/` folder and click the file you want to edit.
2. Click the pencil ✏️ icon (top right of the file view).
3. Make your changes, click **"Commit changes"**, and wait about a minute. Done!

👉 [Full step-by-step guide →](docs/HOW-TO-EDIT.md#️-change-some-words)

---

### 🖼️ Swap a photo

1. Go into the `images/` folder and click on the image you want to replace.
2. Upload a new file with the **exact same name** (this is the secret — same name = automatic swap!).
3. Wait about a minute and refresh your website.

👉 [Full step-by-step guide →](docs/HOW-TO-EDIT.md#️-swap-a-picture)

---

### 👤 Add a teammate

1. Open `content/team.md` and copy an existing teammate block (two lines: their name and their role).
2. Change the two lines to your new teammate's name and role.
3. Upload their photo to `images/team/` named `firstname-lastname.jpg` (all lowercase, with a dash between names).

👉 [Full step-by-step guide →](docs/HOW-TO-EDIT.md#-add-a-team-member)

---

## ✅ Don't worry — you can't permanently break it!

> **If something looks wrong, just undo your last change.**
> Every change you make is saved in the history. You can always go back. Nothing is ever deleted forever. Take a deep breath — you've got this! 😊
